Storage Instructions

KEEP FROZEN

Keep frozen until ready for thawing.

THAW

Always try to thaw in the fridge. Alternatively, thaw in a bowl of warm water. 

STORAGE AND SAFETY

Keep thawed in the fridge for no longer than 48 hours.
